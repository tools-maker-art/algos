🧬 Twin Spy Notes

Longest Common Subsequence

World D Strings

🎮 Objective

Find the length of the longest sequence of letters appearing in order in both strings.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Given strings A,B return length of longest common subsequence.

🎭 Story

Two spies wrote two notes. Find the longest secret phrase whose letters appear (in order) in both.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all subsequences of A and check.

🌱 From brute → recursion

If A[i]==B[j]: 1+LCS(i-1,j-1). Else max(LCS(i-1,j), LCS(i,j-1)).

📊 Complexity

O(n·m) time, O(n·m) or O(min(n,m)) space.

💡 Key takeaway

Match? +1 diagonal. Else max of left/up. The classic 2D string DP.
