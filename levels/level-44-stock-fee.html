💸 The Tax Man

Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock with Transaction Fee

World F Stocks

🎮 Objective

Unlimited trades — but each sale charges a fixed fee.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

profit -= fee on every sell.

🎭 Story

The broker takes a cut on every sale. Trade only when worth it.

Combos.
