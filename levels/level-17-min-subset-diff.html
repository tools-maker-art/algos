⚖️ Smallest Sibling Quarrel

Minimum Subset Sum Difference

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Split the array into two groups so the difference of their sums is as small as possible.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Find subset S1 such that |sum(S1) - sum(S2)| is minimised.

🎭 Story

Two siblings split candy. They’ll cry less if the split is closer to fair.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

All subsets.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Find all reachable subset sums up to total/2; the closest one to total/2 minimises the gap.

🔁 Recursion

Boolean reach as in Subset Sum.

# pseudo
Boolean reach as in Subset Sum.

🧩 Memoization

2D cache.

🧱 Tabulation

dp[k] = can we form k? Then ans = min total − 2k for reachable k.

🚀 Space optimisation

O(sum).
