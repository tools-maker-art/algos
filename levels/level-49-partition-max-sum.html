📦 K-Sized Boxes

Partition Array for Maximum Sum

World G Partition DP

🎮 Objective

Partition array into chunks of length ≤ K. Each chunk becomes max-of-chunk · length. Maximise total.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Maximise sum of (max·len) across all chunks.

🎭 Story

Pack items into boxes of size ≤ K; you can pretend every item in a box is the heaviest one. Maximise total.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all partitions.

🌱 From brute → recursion

dp[i] = max over j=1..K of dp[i-j] + j·max(arr[i-j..i-1]).
