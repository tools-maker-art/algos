🪓 Where to Cut the Stick?

Minimum Cost to Cut a Stick

World G Partition DP

🎮 Objective

Cut a stick at given positions in any order to minimise total cost (cost = current piece length).

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

cuts[] gives positions; choose order to minimise total cut cost.

🎭 Story

A carpenter must cut at given marks. Each cut costs the full piece’s length. Order matters.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every order.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Sort cuts (with 0 and n added). dp(i,j)= min over k of dp(i,k)+dp(k,j) + (cuts[j]-cuts[i]).
