❄️ Falling Snow Path

Minimum/Maximum Falling Path Sum

World B Grid DP

🎮 Objective

Drop a snowflake row-by-row down the grid. Minimise (or maximise) the total of cells you touch.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

From (i,j) you may go to (i+1,j-1), (i+1,j), or (i+1,j+1). Optimise sum.

🎭 Story

A snowflake falls down a grid. Each row it shifts at most one column left or right. Find the cheapest descent.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Recurse from each starting column.

🌱 From brute → recursion

dp(i,j) = grid[i][j] + min( dp(i-1,j-1), dp(i-1,j), dp(i-1,j+1) ).
