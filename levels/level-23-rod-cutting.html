🪵 The Lumberjack&rsquo;s Saw

Rod Cutting

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Cut a rod of length N into pieces to maximise total selling price.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

price[i] = price of a piece of length i+1. Cut rod of length N to max revenue.

🎭 Story

A lumberjack’s rod can be sold whole or chopped. Each length sells at a known price.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every cut combination.

🌱 From brute → recursion

best(N) = max over i in 1..N of price[i-1] + best(N-i). Same as Unbounded Knapsack.
