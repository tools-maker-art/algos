Edit Distance

World D Strings

🎮 Objective

Find the minimum number of single-character edits to turn A into B (insert/delete/replace).

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Levenshtein distance.

🎭 Story

Spell-checker: how many tiny edits turn 'kitten' into 'sitting'?

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all sequences of edits.

🌱 From brute → recursion

edit(i,j) = if A[i]==B[j] → edit(i-1,j-1); else 1 + min( insert, delete, replace ).
