🔍 Hidden Patterns

Distinct Subsequences

World D Strings

🎮 Objective

Count how many distinct subsequences of S equal T.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Return number of distinct ways to delete characters of S leaving exactly T.

🎭 Story

A puzzle book asks: in how many ways can you erase letters from a long word to reveal a shorter target word?

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every subset of S.

🌱 From brute → recursion

dp(i,j): if S[i]==T[j] → dp(i-1,j-1)+dp(i-1,j); else dp(i-1,j).
