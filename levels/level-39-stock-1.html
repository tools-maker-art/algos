💹 Buy Low, Sell High (Once)

Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock I

World F Stocks

🎮 Objective

One buy, one sell. Maximise profit.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

prices[i] = price on day i. Single buy + single sell. Max profit.

🎭 Story

A trader allowed only one trade. Track minimum so far, watch profit grow.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all (buy,sell) pairs (n²).

🌱 From brute → recursion

Track min so far; ans = max(price - min_so_far).
