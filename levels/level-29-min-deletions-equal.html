Min Insertions/Deletions to Equal

World D Strings

🎮 Objective

Convert string A into B using only insertions and deletions, with the fewest operations.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Return min operations (insert in A or delete from A).

🎭 Story

Two notes must match. You can erase letters from A or write new ones. Minimise edits.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Edit-distance-style brute force.

🌱 From brute → recursion

ops = (n - LCS(A,B)) + (m - LCS(A,B)) = n + m − 2·LCS.
