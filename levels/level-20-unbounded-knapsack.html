🔁 Bottomless Coin Cup

Unbounded Knapsack

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Same knapsack — but each item can be picked any number of times.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Maximise value with unlimited copies, weight ≤ W.

🎭 Story

A magic shop: every item is restocked instantly. Pick the best mix.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Recurse: pick or skip; on pick, stay on same i.

🌱 From brute → recursion

best(i,w) = max( best(i-1,w), val[i]+best(i,w-wt[i]) ).

🧱 Tabulation

dp[w] = max over items: dp[w]=max(dp[w], dp[w-wt[i]]+val[i]); iterate w low→high.
