💰 Loot Without Touching

Maximum Sum of Non-Adjacent Elements

World A Basics

🎮 Objective

Pick numbers from an array to maximise sum — but you can’t pick two side-by-side.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Given nums[], maximise sum of a chosen subset where no two chosen indices are adjacent.

🎭 Story

A row of treasure piles. Picking pile i wakes up pile i+1’s guard. Choose wisely!

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try pick / skip at every index. 2ⁿ.

🌱 From brute → recursion

At index i, two options: take nums[i] + best(i-2), or skip = best(i-1). Take the larger.

🔁 Recursion

best(i) = max( nums[i] + best(i-2), best(i-1) ).

# pseudo
best(i) = max( nums[i] + best(i-2), best(i-1) ).

🧩 Memoization

Cache best(i).

🧱 Tabulation

dp[0]=nums[0]; dp[1]=max(nums[0],nums[1]); recurrence onward.

🚀 Space optimisation

Two variables: prev2, prev1.
