💎 Cheapest Treasure Trail

Minimum Path Sum

World B Grid DP

🎮 Objective

Find a path from top-left to bottom-right that minimises the total of cell values.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Each cell has a positive cost. Move Right/Down. Minimise sum of costs along path.

🎭 Story

Each step costs gold. Find the cheapest trail to the treasure room.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all paths.

🌱 From brute → recursion

dp(i,j)=grid[i][j]+min(dp(i-1,j), dp(i,j-1)).

🔁 Recursion

As above. Base: dp(0,0)=grid[0][0].
