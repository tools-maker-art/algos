Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock II

World F Stocks

🎮 Objective

Unlimited trades. Maximise profit.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Buy/sell as many times as you like (one position at a time).

🎭 Story

A day trader sums every uphill segment of the price chart.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all sequences.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Greedy/DP: profit += max(0, prices[i] - prices[i-1]).

🔁 Recursion

DP with state (day, holding).

# pseudo
DP with state (day, holding).

🧩 Memoization

2D cache (n,2).

🧱 Tabulation

dp[i][0]=max(dp[i-1][0], dp[i-1][1]+p[i]); dp[i][1]=max(dp[i-1][1], dp[i-1][0]-p[i]).

🚀 Space optimisation

Two variables (cash, hold).
