🔗 Divisibility Chain

Largest Divisible Subset

World E LIS

🎮 Objective

Find the largest subset such that every pair (a,b) satisfies a%b==0 or b%a==0.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Largest subset where every pair is divisible.

🎭 Story

Chain of magic gears — each gear must divide its neighbour. Build the longest chain.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Enumerate.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Sort. Then it’s LIS-style: dp[i] = max dp[j]+1 where nums[i]%nums[j]==0.
