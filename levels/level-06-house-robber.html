Level 06

🏠 The Polite Robber

House Robber

World A Basics

🎮 Objective

Same as Level 5, told as a heist: maximise loot without robbing two adjacent houses.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Houses in a line, value v[i]. Don’t rob adjacent. Maximise total stolen.

🎭 Story

A robber with manners — he avoids alarming neighbours. Adjacent houses share a wall (and an alarm).

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Pick/skip per house — 2ⁿ.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Take v[i]+best(i-2) or skip best(i-1). Identical to non-adjacent sum, just dressed up.

🔁 Recursion

rob(i) = max( v[i]+rob(i-2), rob(i-1) ).

# pseudo
rob(i) = max( v[i]+rob(i-2), rob(i-1) ).

🧩 Memoization

Cache rob(i).

🧱 Tabulation

Same as Level 5.

🚀 Space optimisation

prev2, prev1.
