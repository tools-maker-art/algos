🎈 Pop the Balloons

Burst Balloons

World G Partition DP

🎮 Objective

Burst balloons in an order that maximises coins (coins = left·me·right at burst time).

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Each balloon has a value. Pop one — earn left·me·right of remaining neighbours.

🎭 Story

Pop balloons cleverly; pop big ones first so they earn more from juicy neighbours.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every permutation.

🌱 From brute → recursion

Add 1 to both ends. dp(i,j) = max over k in (i,j) of dp(i,k)+dp(k,j)+nums[i]·nums[k]·nums[j].
