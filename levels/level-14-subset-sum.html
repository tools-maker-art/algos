🎒 Can the Bag Hit the Target?

Subset Sum

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Can a subset of numbers add up to exactly K?

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Given nums[] and target K, is there a subset whose sum equals K?

🎭 Story

You have a bag of pebbles, each weighing different grams. The shopkeeper asks: “Can you give me exactly K grams?”

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every subset (2ⁿ).

🌱 From brute → recursion

At each item: pick or skip. State = (i, current_sum). Two choices.

🔁 Recursion

canSum(i, target) = canSum(i-1, target) OR canSum(i-1, target - nums[i]).

# pseudo
canSum(i, target) = canSum(i-1, target) OR canSum(i-1, target - nums[i]).

🧩 Memoization

2D cache by (i, target).

🧱 Tabulation

dp[i][k]: can we make k from first i? Fill row-by-row.

🚀 Space optimisation

One row of size K+1, traverse target high→low.
