⚖️ Fair-Share Day

Partition Equal Subset Sum

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Split the array into two groups with equal total.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Can you partition nums[] into two subsets with equal sums?

🎭 Story

Two siblings, one pile of candy. Can they split the pile perfectly evenly?

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try every subset.

🌱 From brute → recursion

If total is odd → impossible. Else target = total/2 → reduces to Subset Sum!

🔁 Recursion

canSum(i, target) — same as Level 14.

# pseudo
canSum(i, target) — same as Level 14.

🧩 Memoization

2D cache.

🧱 Tabulation

Boolean dp[K+1].

🚀 Space optimisation

O(K).
