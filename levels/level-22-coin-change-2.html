🪙 How Many Wallets?

Coin Change II

World C Knapsack

🎮 Objective

Count the number of distinct ways to make amount A.

🧠 Problem (in tiny words)

Return number of combinations (order doesn’t matter) that sum to A.

🎭 Story

A coin collector wants to know in how many distinct ways can the amount be paid.

😵 Brute force (the painful way)

Try all combinations.

🌱 From brute → recursion

ways(A) = sum over coins of ways(A - c) — but iterate coins outer to avoid permutations.

🔁 Recursion

ways(i,A) = ways(i-1,A) + ways(i,A-c[i]).

# pseudo
ways(i,A) = ways(i-1,A) + ways(i,A-c[i]).

🧩 Memoization

2D cache.

🧱 Tabulation

dp[0]=1. For each coin c: for a=c..A: dp[a]+=dp[a-c].

🚀 Space optimisation

O(A).
