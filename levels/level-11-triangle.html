⛰️ The story

Pyramid of numbers. From t[i][j] you can move to (i+1,j) or (i+1,j+1). Min total descent.

what is the recurrence?

The DP recurrence in plain math:

dp(i,j) = t[i][j] + min(dp(i+1,j), dp(i+1,j+1))
